# Cafe-Management-System

Introduction 
CafeEase is a desktop-based cafe management system. It has two types of users: 
Admin and Employee. The admin has control over managing all accounts, 
managing food items and monitoring sales in order history. On the other hand, 
employees focus on ordering food items, generating bills and updating sales in 
order history. Overall, the system simplifies cafe operations, ensures accurate 
billing and makes managing food items and employees more organized and 
efficient. 
 
 
User Story 
Admin can: 
1. View total employees, total orders and total income in Dashboard 
2. Add food items so that employees can take orders from customers 
3. Update the details of food items (Id, Product Name, Category, Price, 
Stock and Description) to keep menu accurate 
4. Delete food items that are no longer offered 
5. Search for food items 
6. Add employees to the system 
7. Update employee details when it is required 
8. Delete employees when he/she is no longer a part of the system 
9. Search for employees 
10. View order history 
 
Employees can: 
1. Take order from customer 
2. Generate bills after taking orders 
3. View order history
